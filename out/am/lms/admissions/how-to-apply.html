How to Apply

Follow each step in order. The entire application process typically takes less than one hour.

01

Explore Programs

~10 min

Visit the Admissions overview page and read through available programs — Diploma, Degree, Masters, or Short Courses. Choose the program that best aligns with your calling and educational background.

  • Read each program description carefully
  • Check duration and curriculum highlights
  • Note the entry requirements for your level
02

Review Requirements

~15 min

Visit the Requirements page and gather all documents listed for your chosen program. Having documents ready before starting the form prevents delays.

  • Scan all documents clearly (PDF or high-res image)
  • Prepare your personal statement in advance
  • Secure two reference letters
03

Create Your LMS Account

~5 min

Register for an LMS account. Select your intended program level during registration — this links your profile to the correct admissions track.

  • Use a permanent email address
  • Choose a secure password (min. 8 characters)
  • Select your degree type accurately
04

Submit Your Application

~20 min

Once logged in, navigate to Admissions → Apply. Complete all form sections and upload the required documents. Review everything before submitting.

  • Double-check all personal details
  • Upload documents in the correct format
  • Submit before the intake deadline
05

Await Review

5–7 business days

Our admissions committee reviews all applications within 5–7 business days. You will receive an email notification with your status and next steps.

  • Check your email (including spam/junk folder)
  • Log in to your LMS account to track status
  • Contact the admissions office if no response after 7 days
06

Receive Decision & Enroll

~2 min

If approved, you will receive an acceptance letter and enrollment instructions. Log in and follow the steps to confirm your enrollment and access your courses.

  • Complete enrollment within the deadline provided
  • Review your course schedule
  • Attend orientation if scheduled
