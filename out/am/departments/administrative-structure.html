Administrative Structure

Full hierarchical structure from HQ to local Wereda offices

Structure Overview

The ministry is organized in a tiered model to ensure accountability and support at every level. Starting from the central headquarters, every layer is designed to provide effective resource distribution, monitoring, and capacity building to local communities through dioceses, zones, districts, and Weredas.

Hierarchy Structure

A clear chain of command that delivers consistent ministry support from national leadership to local outreach.

🏢

Headquarters (HQ)

Central decision-making office that sets strategy, policy, and operational direction for the entire ministry.

⛪

Diocese

Regional leadership tier providing guidance and support to zone directors and ensuring mission alignment.

📍

Zone

A collection of districts coordinating training, finance, and program rollout across local areas.

🏘️

District

Supervises Wereda offices, tracks goals, and delivers localized training and resources.

🌱

Wereda

Grassroots team at district level that connects local churches, schools, and outreach efforts.

Roles & Responsibilities

👤

HQ Administration

Strategy, policy, finance, and national-level program oversight.

👥

Diocese Leaders

Regional coordination, compliance, and resource allocation for zones.

🔧

Zone Managers

Implementation of projects, capacity-building, and performance monitoring.

🏛️

District Coordinators

Site visit planning, local coaching, and operational support for Wereda offices.

📊

Wereda Teams

Community engagement, beneficiary follow-up, and local reporting.
